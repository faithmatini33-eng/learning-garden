Turn 8 — Motion & Sound

The motion & sound kit

Every animation runs live below with its exact spec, and every card has a hear it button (sounds are synthesized in sounds.js — no audio files needed). Rules: motion is short (<800ms), springy, and always tied to something the kid did; wrong answers get a gentle nudge and a soft "hmm", never harsh buzzers or red flashes.
